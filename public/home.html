Photo Taker

Description

Take a picture on raspberrypi using click the Capture button. Within a few seconds, the picture will be displayed below. If repeated snapshot requests are sent, a prompt saying 'wait a few seconds' appears. You can take the screenshot after about 3 seoconds. The images will be saved locally in raspberrypi 'raspberrypi-photo-taker/public/images' folder.
